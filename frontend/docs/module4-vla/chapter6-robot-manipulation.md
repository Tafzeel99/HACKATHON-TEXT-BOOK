# Chapter 6: Robot Manipulation

This chapter covers the robot's ability to interact physically with its environment through manipulation, including grasping and placing objects.

## Key Concepts

*   **Manipulator Kinematics**: Understanding the robot arm's movement and joint configurations.
*   **Grasping and Placement**: Strategies and algorithms for successfully grasping and placing objects.
*   **ROS 2 MoveIt Integration**: Conceptual integration with MoveIt for advanced manipulation planning.
*   **Manipulation Planning**: How fine-grained manipulation plans are generated and executed.
